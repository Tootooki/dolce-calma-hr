About Us

About Dolce Calma

We are Dolce Calma, a growing USA-based eCommerce brand selling premium Dubai chocolates, pistachio creams, and luxury Middle Eastern confections on Amazon and Shopify. We need a sharp, analytical PPC coordinator to take ownership of our Amazon advertising strategy and guide our team with clear, daily action plans.

The Role

What Makes This Role Unique

This is not an execution role — we have people doing the hands-on campaign work. We need a strategic coordinator who will:

  • Analyze campaign data and identify what's working and what's not
  • Create clear, written DAILY ACTION PLANS telling the team exactly what to do
  • Decide which campaigns to create, pause, or adjust — and communicate WHY
  • Set bid strategies, budget allocations, and targeting approaches
  • Think about the big picture while also catching the small details
What You'll Be Doing

Your Responsibilities

  • Develop and oversee Amazon PPC strategy across Sponsored Products, Brands, and Display
  • Analyze campaign performance data daily and create written action plans
  • Manage keyword research, harvesting, and negative keyword strategy
  • Set and optimize bids, budgets, and placement modifiers
  • Structure campaigns and ad groups for maximum efficiency and scalability
  • Monitor ACoS, TACoS, ROAS, and other key advertising metrics
  • Coordinate with the team — assign specific tasks with clear deadlines
  • Create portfolio structures for organized campaign management
  • Conduct competitor advertising analysis and market research
  • Produce weekly and monthly PPC performance reports
  • Identify new advertising opportunities (video ads, DSP, AMC)
  • Optimize product targeting and category targeting campaigns
