About Us

About Dolce Calma

We are Dolce Calma, a growing USA-based eCommerce brand specializing in premium Dubai chocolates, pistachio creams, kataifi, and luxury Middle Eastern sweets. We sell on multiple platforms including Amazon, Shopify, eBay, Walmart, and TikTok Shop. We're looking for an experienced e-commerce manager to take ownership of our multi-platform operations.

What You'll Be Doing

Your Responsibilities

  • Manage day-to-day operations across Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Shopify, and TikTok Shop
  • Create, optimize, and maintain product listings across all platforms
  • Monitor inventory levels and coordinate restocking with the team
  • Analyze sales data, trends, and marketplace analytics to drive decisions
  • Handle pricing strategy, competitor analysis, and promotional planning
  • Oversee customer service responses and maintain high seller ratings
  • Coordinate with the design team on listing images and A+ content
  • Manage FBA/FBM logistics, shipping plans, and fulfillment operations
  • Track and resolve account health issues, policy violations, and case logs
  • Create weekly performance reports with KPIs and actionable insights
  • Research and launch new products or enter new marketplace categories

Platforms You'll Work With

  • Amazon — Seller Central, FBA, advertising, A+ content, brand registry
  • Shopify — Store management, themes, apps, checkout optimization
  • eBay — Listings, promoted listings, store management
  • Walmart — Marketplace seller portal, listing optimization
  • TikTok Shop — Product listings, affiliate programs, shop management
