What You'll Be Doing

Daily Responsibilities (There's a LOT to Do)

  • Plan, create, and schedule daily posts across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, and Facebook
  • Develop and maintain a monthly content calendar aligned with promotions and seasons
  • Write compelling captions, hashtags, and copy for every post
  • Engage with followers — respond to comments, DMs, and mentions within minutes
  • Research and capitalize on trending topics, sounds, and formats daily
  • Identify and coordinate with micro-influencers and UGC creators
  • Monitor competitor social accounts and industry trends
  • Analyze performance metrics and create weekly/monthly reports
  • A/B test content formats, posting times, and hashtag strategies
  • Coordinate with the design and video team for visual assets
  • Manage community building — grow followers, engagement, and brand loyalty
  • Set up and manage Pinterest boards/pins for SEO-driven traffic
  • Create and manage YouTube content (Shorts, long-form, community posts)
  • Cross-post and repurpose content across platforms efficiently

Platforms You'll Manage

  • Instagram — Feed posts, Stories, Reels, IGTV, community management
  • TikTok — Short-form viral content, trends, hashtag challenges
  • YouTube — Shorts, product videos, channel management
  • Pinterest — Pins, boards, idea pins, SEO-driven food content
  • Facebook — Page management, groups, marketplace
